Break Aging
All exceptions open as of the report date, bucketed by business days unresolved
Read this with context: this is a static, one-shot historical dataset with no daily break-resolution mechanism, so early-window breaks simply keep aging for the rest of the 6-month run — that's why 15+ business days dominates. A live daily system, where breaks get matched and cleared as later bank postings arrive, would show a materially healthier distribution. The aging mechanism (bucketing, SLA threshold, cash-at-risk flag) is real and tested; this particular shape is an artifact of the demo data, not the engine.
